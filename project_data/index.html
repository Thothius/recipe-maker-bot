About Cookbooker

Cookbooker is a voice-controlled recipe assistant powered by OpenAI's GPT-4 Realtime API.

Simply speak your ingredients and amounts naturally - our AI captures everything and builds structured recipes automatically.

Create recipes hands-free, get instant nutrition analysis, and save your culinary creations effortlessly.
